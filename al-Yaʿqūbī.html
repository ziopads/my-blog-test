Al-Yaʿqūbī

Al-Yaʿqūbī is a Islamic Geographer, author and the is the first historian of world culture in the Abbasid Caliphate. Al-Ya qubi wrote a book called Taʾrik ibn Wadih, which is divided into two different books. The book talks about islamic people and non-islamic people and also focuses on islamic religion, literature, and also includes Greek philosophy. The book also talks about Islamic history back during the 800's and also talks about the major islamic cities.
